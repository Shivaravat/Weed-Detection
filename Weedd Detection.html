Weed Treatment Guide

Weed Types & Identification

Common Weed Types in India:

  • Grassy Weeds: Gulli Danda, Jangli Jowar
  • Broadleaf Weeds: Bathua, Chulai, Kulfa
  • Sedges: Motha, Deela
  • Parasitic Weeds: Amarbel

Treatment Methods

Chemical Control:

  • Pre-emergence herbicides
  • Post-emergence selective herbicides
  • Systemic herbicides for perennial weeds

Organic Methods:

  • Mulching with organic materials
  • Manual weeding
  • Crop rotation
  • Bio-herbicides
